    Our Story

    Anino Inc. (formerly Anino Playlab, Anino Games, Inc.) is the first and leading game developer in the Philippines with more than 60 games under its belt since it started in 2002.

    A multi-awarded studio and a pioneer in the Philippines, they have successfully worked as a 3rd party developer with top-tier publishers such as Electronic Arts, Activision, Namco, Game House, Disney and Big Fish Games among others.
